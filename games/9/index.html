Sequences

A board with 9 tiles. A sequence of CSS transforms. If the starting point is the center tile and facing right, what would be the ending tile after executing all the transforms?

Easy

  • translate(-50%)
  • translate(100%, -50%)
  • rotate(90deg)
  • translate3d(50%, 0, 0)
  • rotate(90deg)
  • translate(50%, -50%)
  • rotate(45deg)
  • translate3d(0, 0, 0)
  • rotate(0.125turn)
  • translateX(50%)
