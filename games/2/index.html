Infinite Ring

Hidden in this ring, there are 6 CSS terms. The end letters of each term are the beginning of the following term. The last one's ending matches the first one's beginning. The numbers on the tiles indicate the beginning of the term. Each tile can hold between 2 and 4 characters (you must write the dashes but they don't count as a character.)
